Khon kaen

Khon Kaen Gems

Khon Kaen, a hub of Isaan heritage, offers a blend of ancient Khmer temples, striking architecture, vibrant festivals, and serene landscapes, making it a captivating destination for cultural exploration in Thailand's North-East.

Top Attractions

Wat Nong Waeng

Wat Nong Waeng is a prominent temple in Khon Kaen, known for its striking nine-tiered stupa and serene atmosphere. This spiritual site provides visitors with panoramic views of the surrounding area, making it a popular destination for both worshippers and tourists alike.

Khon Kaen National Museum

Khon Kaen National Museum showcases the rich history and culture of the Isan region through its diverse exhibits, including artifacts from ancient civilizations, local art, and ethnographic displays. The museum offers an insightful experience for those looking to learn more about the area's heritage.
