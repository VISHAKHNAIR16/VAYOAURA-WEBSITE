ABOUT VAYOAURA - VA

Our Story

Founded in 2024, VayoAura is a dynamic and innovative travel B2B company with a fresh perspective on the travel industry. Led by young, talented owners who are dedicated to customer satisfaction, quality of service, and timely execution, we are passionate about creating unique, unforgettable experiences that resonate with travelers of all ages. Our mission is to redefine travel by offering tailor-made and immersive programs that cater to the diverse interests and passions of our clients, making everyone feel youthful and invigorated.

Based in Bangkok, VayoAura is dedicated to providing cutting-edge destination management services across South East Asia. We leverage our deep local knowledge and a network of trusted partners to deliver exceptional travel experiences that inspire and delight. Our commitment extends beyond our clients as we aim to foster growth and success for everyone we work with.

Our team has extensive experience in managing local operations and quickly addressing on-ground issues, allowing us to respond effectively to our clients' ever-changing needs and the challenges that may arise during their journeys
