Chiang Mai

The Hidden Charms of Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai is a cultural gem, offering ancient temples like Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, vibrant festivals, stunning nature at Doi Inthanon, and unforgettable experiences at places like the Elephant Nature Park and Bua Thong Waterfall.

Top Attractions

Wat Phra That Doi Suthep

Wat Phra That Doi Suthep, perched on a mountain overlooking Chiang Mai, is a revered temple known for its stunning golden chedi and panoramic city views. This sacred site is a key pilgrimage destination and offers a peaceful, spiritual experience amidst its beautiful surroundings.

Wat Chedi Luang

Wat Chedi Luang, located in Chiang Mai, Thailand, is a historic temple known for its towering ancient chedi, once the tallest structure in the Lanna Kingdom. The temple's serene atmosphere and impressive ruins make it a key cultural and spiritual landmark in the city.

Bua Thong Waterfall

Bua Thong Waterfall, located in Chiang Mai, Thailand, is a unique "sticky" limestone waterfall known for its natural grip, allowing visitors to easily climb its cascading tiers. Surrounded by lush jungle, this serene spot offers a refreshing escape and a fun, hands-on experience with nature.
