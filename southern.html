Southern Vietnam

"Soul of Vietnam" : Southern Vietnam

"Southern Vietnam is a vibrant mix of bustling cities, lush landscapes, and cultural treasures. Explore Ho Chi Minh City’s dynamic energy, cruise the serene Mekong Delta, or relax on the pristine beaches of Phú Quốc. From floating markets to historic landmarks like the Cu Chi Tunnels, Southern Vietnam offers a unique blend of modern life and timeless traditions."

Top Attractions

Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)

Ho Chi Minh City, formerly known as Saigon, is the bustling heart of southern Vietnam. A dynamic blend of modern skyscrapers, historic landmarks like the Notre Dame Cathedral and War Remnants Museum, vibrant markets, and a buzzing nightlife scene make it a captivating destination. It's a city of energy, culture, and endless discoveries.

Mekong Delta

The Mekong Delta, known as Vietnam’s "Rice Bowl," is a lush and vibrant region in central Vietnam. Famous for its winding waterways, floating markets, and picturesque rice paddies, it offers a glimpse into rural Vietnamese life. Visitors can explore by boat, savor fresh tropical fruits, and experience the charm of riverside villages.

Phu Quoc Island

Phu Quoc Island, a tropical paradise in central Vietnam, is celebrated for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush forests. Perfect for relaxation and adventure, visitors can enjoy snorkeling, exploring national parks, and savoring fresh seafood. It's an idyllic getaway for nature and beach lovers.
