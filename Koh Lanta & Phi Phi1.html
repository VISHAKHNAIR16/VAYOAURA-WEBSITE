Koh Lanta & Phi Phi Islands

Koh Lanta & Phi Phi: Escape to Serenity

Explore Koh Lanta's serene beaches and landscapes, then dive into the vibrant marine life and stunning cliffs of Phi Phi Islands, including lively nightlife and unforgettable beach parties.

Top Attractions

Viking Cave

Viking Cave, located on the eastern side of Thailand's Phi Phi Leh Island, is a fascinating sea cave known for its ancient wall paintings resembling Viking ships. Nestled amid towering limestone cliffs, the cave is also famous for its swiftlet bird nests, which are harvested for bird's nest soup, a local delicacy. Accessible by boat, Viking Cave offers visitors a glimpse into both the island's natural beauty and cultural heritage, making it a unique destination in the Andaman Sea.

Maya Bay

Maya Bay, famously featured in the movie "The Beach," is a breathtaking bay surrounded by towering limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters. Located on Koh Phi Phi Leh, it is renowned for its stunning scenery and vibrant marine life, making it a must-visit for snorkeling and photography.

Koh Lanta Full Moon Party

Koh Lanta Full Moon Party is a lively beach celebration held monthly on Koh Lanta, featuring music, dancing, and vibrant festivities under the full moon. This fun-filled event attracts locals and travelers alike, creating a festive atmosphere that showcases the island's nightlife and communal spirit.
