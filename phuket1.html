PHUKET

"Phuket Paradise" : Phuket

Phuket, is a modern city with bustling street life, beaches and grand temples such as Chaithararam Temple - Wat Chalong , Karon Beach , Old Phuket Town .

Top Attractions

Karon Beach

Karon Beach is a picturesque stretch of sand in Phuket, known for its clear blue waters and soft golden sands. Less crowded than nearby beaches, it offers a relaxed atmosphere perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying stunning sunsets.

Chaithararam Temple - Wat Chalong

Chaithararam Temple (Wat Chalong) is the largest and most important Buddhist temple in Phuket, renowned for its stunning architecture and serene surroundings. Visitors can explore beautifully decorated halls, admire intricate sculptures, and participate in local religious practices.

Old Phuket Town

Old Phuket Town is a charming historical area filled with colorful Sino-Portuguese architecture, vibrant markets, and quaint cafes.
