KOH SAMUI

"Koh Samui Escapade" : KOH SAMUI

Koh Samui, is a modern city with bustling street life, beaches and grand temples such as Big Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Yai) , Lamai Beach , Na Mueang Waterfall .

Top Attractions

Big Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Yai)

Big Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Yai) is a stunning landmark in Koh Samui, featuring a massive 12-meter golden Buddha statue perched on a platform. This spiritual site offers visitors a serene atmosphere and panoramic views of the surrounding area, making it a must-visit for both worshippers and tourists.

Lamai Beach

Lamai Beach is a beautiful stretch of sand on Koh Samui, known for its laid-back vibe and crystal-clear waters. With a mix of lively beach bars and quiet spots for relaxation, itâ€™s perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying stunning sunsets.

Na Mueang Waterfall

Na Muang Waterfall is a picturesque natural attraction on Koh Samui, featuring two stunning cascades surrounded by lush tropical greenery. Visitors can swim in the refreshing pools at the base of the falls or hike along the trails for a closer look at this beautiful oasis.
